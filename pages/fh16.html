Volvo FH16. Powered for heavy transport.

Engineered for the most demanding tasks, the Volvo FH16 delivers the superior power and torque necessary to overcome
challenges that leave other trucks behind. With the high-efficiency D17 engine and enhanced cabin comfort,
it is the ultimate tool for maximizing your professional productivity.

Applications: Heavy transport assignments, forestry and timber transportation, mining and quarry transportation

Power: 540-780 hp, dependent on market

Price: From 160.000 €

Availability: Asia/Pacific, Europe, Middle East, Africa

A truck powered for your productivity

Combining high-performance engineering with a new standard of cab comfort, the Volvo FH16 is built for those who demand more from their drive.
It's a truck that stands in a class of its own across every version. Discover its exceptional features in our visual gallery.

Optimized vision with adaptive high beam

The signature black-themed headlamps are a unique identifier for the Volvo FH16, blending high-end design with superior performance.
These LED units deliver a crisp, distinct profile and outstanding light output.

Enhance your safety with the adaptive high beam option, which intelligently optimizes your field of vision without compromising the comfort and safety of oncoming traffic.

Heavy-duty bumper

Maximize your vehicle's protection with a front bumper built for extreme conditions.
Crafted from 3-mm high-tensile steel, it acts as a fortified barrier against debris and collisions, saving you from costly headlight repairs and body damage.

It's a component that is every bit as durable as its tough exterior suggests.

I-Shift with crawler gears

Managing extreme weight from a dead stop requires peak performance from your traction and clutch systems.

I-Shift with crawler gears is engineered to optimize startability and shield your clutch from the intense pressure of heavy-duty hauling, ensuring a smooth and efficient departure every time.

Volvo FH16

Iconic by design and relentless in performance. With a staggering 780 hp and a wide spectrum of options, this isn't just a truck—it's an expert tool built for your unique challenges.

From heavy-duty axles to premium interior finishes and energy-efficient disc brakes, every detail is under your command. It's time to shape your power.

Volvo Dynamic Steering

From precise low-speed positioning with heavy loads to high-speed stability on uneven roads, Volvo Dynamic Steering is your constant support.

It functions like a steady hand, absorbing road shocks and counteracting ruts to make your drive effortless and stress-free.

Save fuel with I-See

To reduce operational costs and lower your carbon footprint, I-See optimizes every aspect of the powertrain—from gear shifting to engine stops—based on the path forward.

It accounts for terrain elevation, curves, and current traffic conditions in real-time, ensuring that your Volvo FH operates at the pinnacle of fuel efficiency for every single mile.

Camera Monitor System

The Camera Monitor System is engineered to reduce air resistance and optimize energy efficiency.
Beyond its aerodynamic benefits, it grants the driver a clearer field of vision and includes smart
digital features that surpass the limitations of standard glass.

Nevertheless, for hauling longer trailers or combinations, standard side mirrors are still recommended for optimal maneuverability.

Infotainment and audio

The Volvo FH16 elevates life on the road with a user-centric dashboard and a sophisticated infotainment system.
Complemented by a choice of two superior audio levels, the cabin transforms into a sanctuary of comfort.

It's a space where technology and ergonomics meet to enhance both your driving hours and your downtime.
