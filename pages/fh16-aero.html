Volvo FH16 Aero

Boasting a commanding presence and a highly aerodynamic profile, this vehicle is engineered for ultimate efficiency.
Innovative drag-free disc brakes eliminate unnecessary friction, helping to significantly lower your fuel costs
while minimizing your environmental impact. It is the perfect synergy of robust power and uncompromising sustainability,
designed for the modern road.

Powered for performance and efficiency

The D17 stands as the pinnacle of Volvo powertrain engineering. Beyond its record-breaking power,
it's the fusion of high torque and fuel economy that makes the FH16 Aero the ultimate tool for
heavy-duty hauls at sustained high speeds.

Up to 780 hp

The D17 is a 17-litre inline-six powerhouse, engineered for reduced weight and maximum thermal efficiency.
Tailor your performance with three output options: 600 hp, 700 hp, or the record-breaking 780 hp.

More efficiency and higher torque

The FH16 Aero powertrain lineup redefines heavy-duty performance. Delivering up to 3800 Nm of torque and superior
braking force at low RPMs, it allows you to configure a highly efficient
system that significantly lowers operational fuel costs.

Perfect gear changes with I-Shift

Experience the power of millisecond precision. With I-Shift, every gear change is perfectly timed
for maximum smoothness and efficiency. Choose from specialized software packages to
optimize your powertrain for the heaviest loads.

Volvo Dynamic Steering: a steady hand

Effortless precision for heavy-duty tasks. Volvo Dynamic Steering acts like a steady hand,
providing total control during low-speed maneuvers while
seamlessly integrating with your advanced driver support systems.

Our History

As part of the completion of the final project for the 121 “Software Engineering” program, the primary focus was on developing the software architecture and writing the code for the web application. To ensure a modern and user-friendly experience (UX), the graphical interface of Volvo's official website was chosen as a visual reference for the client-side. I independently implemented this interface from scratch (writing markup, styles, and scripts).
