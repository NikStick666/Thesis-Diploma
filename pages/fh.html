Volvo FH: The definitive legend of long-distance transport.

A masterpiece of long-distance engineering, the Volvo FH is designed to provide unparalleled safety and ergonomics for long-stretch hauls.
Loaded with sophisticated digital systems to boost productivity, it offers a wide spectrum of sustainable electric, gas, and efficient diesel powertrains.

This truck is more than a vehicle; it's a high-tech workplace that turns driving into a premium experience right from day one.

Volvo FH

Applications: Long-haul and regional-haul transport assignments, building and construction transport, forestry and timber transport, mining and quarry transport

Power: 420-540 hp

Price: From 120.000 €

Availability: Asia/Pacific, Europe, Middle East, Africa and Latin America

The "Home Away From Home"

A distinctive headlamp shape and an upright cab profile, easy to identify from a distance.
A range of comfortable cabs packed with solutions that can make your life on the road even better.

Engineered for Aerodynamic Excellence

Features such as the LED lighting, reduced panel gaps, and the Camera Monitor System are key elements of the Volvo FH's efficient design,
working together to lower air resistance and save energy.

Moreover, the inclusion of adaptive high beams allows for optimized night vision without blinding oncoming traffic, significantly enhancing safety on the road.

The perfect cab climate

During your journey, a high-precision climate system manages air distribution within the Volvo FH cab for maximum comfort.
Once the engine is switched off for a break, I-Park Cool takes over, utilizing independent battery energy to provide
a comfortable sleeping environment through the entire night without the need for engine idling.

Everyday comfort

Life in a Volvo FH cab is engineered for absolute convenience during long hauls.
You are provided with ample storage space, twin refrigeration units for fresh supplies, and an integrated microwave to heat up your snacks whenever needed.

And, naturally, the exceptionally comfortable bed serves as a sanctuary for a restful night’s sleep.

Make the Volvo FH your perfect drive

Whether your Volvo FH is deployed for long-distance logistics or demanding construction sites,
the synergy of I-Shift and Volvo Dynamic Steering guarantees flawless gear transitions and exceptionally sharp, effortless handling.

We invite you to explore the gallery below for an in-depth look at these flagship technologies and the full suite of specialized options available for your vehicle.

I-Shift

Tailor your drive with I-Shift transmissions, designed to master everything from highway transport to specialized heavy hauling.
The I-Shift Dual Clutch stands out by delivering seamless gear changes with zero power interruption.

Equipped with crawler gears, this system masters extreme weight from a standstill without sacrificing the fuel-saving benefits required for high-speed operations.

Volvo Dynamic Steering

Volvo Dynamic Steering (VDS) functions as a sophisticated assistive force, acting like a steady hand to simplify handling and significantly reduce physical strain.
Beyond providing a smoother drive, it enhances the capabilities of your driver support systems by offering active steering corrections across diverse driving scenarios.

Furthermore, the system uniquely provides the option to navigate your truck using a dedicated remote control for maximum convenience.

Tandem Axle Lift

Maximize your operational versatility with the Tandem Axle Lift, designed to lower energy consumption and improve tire life.
By allowing you to lift a drive axle, this feature transforms your truck's performance: you get the heavy-duty power of a tandem drive combined with the agility and cost-effectiveness of a single-drive setup.

It is the perfect solution for tasks where you drive heavy in one direction and empty in the other.

Our History

As part of the completion of the final project for the 121 “Software Engineering” program, the primary focus was on developing the software architecture and writing the code for the web application. To ensure a modern and user-friendly experience (UX), the graphical interface of Volvo's official website was chosen as a visual reference for the client-side.
