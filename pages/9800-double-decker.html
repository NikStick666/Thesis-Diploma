Volvo 9800 Double Decker Euro 6

Double the deck, triple the advantage. The Volvo 9800 Double Decker is a masterclass in high-capacity engineering, designed to deliver a premium experience for drivers and a sanctuary for passengers. Powered by our most refined Euro 6 engine, it merges massive payload capabilities with surgical fuel efficiency. Whether you're conquering long-haul intercity routes or high-end tourism, it's a platform built for your most ambitious operational logic. Performance, scaled.

Passenger capacity: Up to 68 seats

Power output: 500 hp

Length: 15.0 m

Permitted GVW: 24,750 kg

The number one double-decker

Dominance through dimension. The Volvo 9800 Double Decker stands alone in its class, merging premium sanctuary-level comfort with an expansive 68-passenger capacity.

Driven by a class-leading 500 hp engine, it is the definitive powerhouse for high-velocity, high-volume transit. Beyond raw performance, it is the only double-decker engineered with an integrated wheelchair ramp, proving that true luxury is inclusive. Built for endurance, optimized for profit.

Fuel efficiency and biofuel

Squeezing performance from every drop. Our redesigned 13-litre Euro 6 driveline integrates a faster rear axle ratio to keep RPMs low and efficiency high. By moving to an electrical cooling architecture and a streamlined engine layout, we've cut fuel consumption by 9%.

It's a cleaner, smarter way to move, with full compatibility for HVO to minimize your environmental footprint. Speed, optimized; waste, deleted.

Volvo Connect

One interface, infinite optimization. Volvo Connect is the central nervous system of your fleet, merging real-time telemetry with strategic service tools. From fuel-saving algorithms to proactive uptime monitoring, we've unified the entire world of Volvo Bus Services into a single, high-velocity portal. Command your fleet, amplify your profit.

Zone management

Beyond standard geofencing lies Volvo Zone Management. It's a context-aware protocol that transforms geographical coordinates into active vehicle logic.

By deploying Safety Zones, you can hardcode speed limits into specific urban polygons—ensuring the bus automatically respects the safety of school zones or high-traffic pedestrian areas. Precision control, enforced by location.

Vehicle Status

Turn data into decisions, instantly. In the Volvo ecosystem, every dashboard warning is a high-priority event that triggers an immediate notification to your control center.

This proactive telemetry allows you to triage issues remotely, reducing downtime and maintaining fleet velocity. Whether completing the shift or returning to base, you're always in command. Predictive uptime at its peak.

Workshop services

Even sourcing the right spare parts is now an integrated online experience. The workshop, reimagined.
