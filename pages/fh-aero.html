Shaped for efficiency

Innovative elements such as the LED headlamps, minimized body gaps, and the advanced Camera Monitor System
are prime examples of the Volvo FH's aerodynamic efficiency, effectively reducing air resistance and energy consumption.

Furthermore, the inclusion of adaptive high beams ensures superior nighttime visibility by automatically adjusting the light
pattern, enhancing safety without blinding other road users.

The perfect cab climate

The Volvo FH features a meticulously calibrated climate management system that maintains fresh air flow
during every mile of your journey. To maximize driver recovery during stationary periods, I-Park Cool offers
an intelligent solution: it provides consistent cooling or heating during overnight breaks powered solely by the vehicle's batteries.

This not only ensures a high-quality rest but also eliminates fuel consumption and noise associated with running the engine at night.

Everyday comfort

Engineered for seamless daily routines, the Volvo FH cab provides a highly organized living environment.
With generous storage solutions, dual refrigeration units, and an integrated microwave, everything you need is within reach.

A superior bed ensures that your recovery time is as productive as your driving time.

Make the Volvo FH your perfect drive

From high-speed highways to rugged construction environments, the Volvo FH delivers unrivaled performance

I-Shift

Customization is at the heart of I-Shift, offering a range of gearboxes tailored to match the specific needs of long-haul,
construction, and specialized heavy transport operations. The Dual Clutch variant redefines smoothness by shifting gears without losing power, ensuring maximum productivity.

Additionally, the inclusion of crawler gears allows operators to handle the heaviest
loads with ease from a standstill, all while maintaining the fuel-saving benefits of the transmission during high-speed highway driving.

Volvo Dynamic Steering

Experience unparalleled stability with Volvo Dynamic Steering, a system engineered to provide a steady hand at the wheel, making every maneuver easier while minimizing driver fatigue.
This technology integrates seamlessly with active safety features to provide corrective steering in critical situations.

For specialized operations, VDS even offers a remote-control steering option, allowing for precise positioning from outside the cab.

Tandem Axle Lift

The Tandem Axle Lift redefines efficiency by merging the best of two worlds.
It provides the massive traction and load-bearing strength of a tandem axle setup, yet allows for the precision and energy savings of a single drive by simply raising the axle when needed.

The result is a tighter turning circle and a smoother, more efficient drive that adapts instantly to your load conditions.
